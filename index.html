Julia Vaz

Multimedia Climate and Science Reporter

I’m a journalist covering the social impacts and science behind climate change. My passion is using multimedia and storytelling to make climate change stories accessible and personal. I was most recently a reporting intern at New Hampshire Public Radio where I covered climate and environment for the By Degrees team. You can also find my writing at Heatmap News and Inside Climate News.

I graduated from Brown University in 2025 with degrees in Political Science and Modern Culture & Media. While at Brown, I was Managing Editor of Newsroom and Vice President of The Brown Daily Herald. Right now, I’m at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology pursuing a graduate degree in Science Writing.

You can reach me julisvaaz[at]gmail[dot]com. You can also find me on LinkedIn and on Goodreads.
